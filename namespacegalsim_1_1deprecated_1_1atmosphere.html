Return the atmospheric modulation transfer function for long exposures with Kolmogorov turbulence as a NumPy array.

Parameters:
array_shapethe NumPy array shape desired for the array view of the ImageViewD.
dxgrid spacing of PSF in real space units
lam_over_r0lambda / r0 in the physical units adopted for dx (user responsible for consistency), where r0 is the Fried parameter. The FWHM of the Kolmogorov PSF is ~0.976 lambda/r0 (e.g., Racine 1996, PASP 699, 108). Typical values for the Fried parameter are on the order of 10 cm for most observatories and up to 20 cm for excellent sites. The values are usually quoted at lambda = 500 nm and r0 depends weakly on wavelength [r0 ~ lambda^(-6/5)].
