Detailed Description

Linear interpolant.

The linear interpolant is a poor choice for FFT-based operations on SBInterpolatedImage, as it rings to high frequencies. (See Bernstein & Gruen 2012 for details.) This objection does not apply when shooting photons, in which case the linear interpolant is quite efficient (but not necessarily the best choice in terms of accuracy).
